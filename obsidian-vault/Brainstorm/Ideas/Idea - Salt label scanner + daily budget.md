# Idea - Salt label scanner + daily budget

## Solves
- [[Problem - Hard to keep salt intake low]]

## Who is the user?
- [[People - Health-conscious busy professionals]]

## Smallest version
- Take a photo of a nutrition label
- OCR "salt" / "sodium" value
- Convert to daily usage (e.g., 5.8g salt/day guideline)
- Simple dashboard: "today used" / "remaining"

## Distribution
- SEO: "salt to sodium converter", "how much salt per day"
- TikTok/Shorts: quick label scans

## Monetization
- Freemium
- Paid: history, reminders, meal templates, export for doctor
